Algos and Data Structures Practiced:
- Vector (arrays)
- Map/unordered map (hash table)
- Linked lists
- Primitive Types
- Strings
- Stacks 
- Two pointer approach
- Slow fast pointer approach
- Binary search
- Binary Tree
    - pre-order traversal: visit root, traverse left subtree, traverse right subtree
    - in-order traversal: traverse left subtree, vist root, traverse right subtree
    - post-order traversal: traverse left subtree, traverse right subtree, vist root
    - level-order traversal: left to right starting from root and moving to next level down
- Dynamic Programming
    - Memoization (top-down)
    - Tabulation (bottom-up)
